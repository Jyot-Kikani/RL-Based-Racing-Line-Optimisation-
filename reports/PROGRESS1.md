## 🎨 Visual & Technical Enhancements

The environment has been upgraded with a suite of "premium" features to improve both performance and aesthetics:

1.  **Super-Sampling Anti-Aliasing (SSAA):**
    - The environment now renders at a higher internal resolution before downscaling.
    - **Result:** Eliminates "pixel jitter" and provides smooth, sub-pixel car movement.
2.  **Visual Smoothing (EMA):**
    - Implemented Exponential Moving Average for the car's heading and sensor rays.
    - **Result:** Silky-smooth visual transitions that don't lag behind the underlying physics.
3.  **Mercedes F1 Custom Sprite:**
